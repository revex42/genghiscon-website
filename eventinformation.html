What happens if the con doesn't run in 2024?

If GenghisCon needs to be cancelled, all tickets brought through grenadine will be refunded on request via email to committee@genghiscon.org (provide your name and ticket number), or honoured as 2025 tickets.

If we need to cancel the convention, the AGM will be held online, and information will be provided in advance.
