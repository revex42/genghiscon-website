What happens if the con doesn't run in 2023?

If GenghisCon needs to be cancelled because of government or health recommendations, all tickets brought through grenadine will be refunded on request via email to genghisconperth@gmail.com (provide your name), or honoured as 2024 tickets.

If we need to cancel the con, the AGM will be held online, and information will be provided in advance.
